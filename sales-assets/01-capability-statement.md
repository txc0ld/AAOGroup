## Secure AI operations for Australian businesses

We install controlled AI operators inside Australian SMBs — with approval gates, logs, and data boundaries. We are not a chatbot vendor and not a strategy consultancy. We deliver one working workflow at a time, governed end-to-end, and we run it as a managed service after pilot.

---

### What we do

- Install narrow AI workflows for admin-heavy operations: lead intake, quote preparation, inbox triage, SOP retrieval, and weekly reporting.
- Wrap every workflow in a human approval queue, structured logs, and a defined risk tier — the controls are the product, not an afterthought.
- Run the workflow on your behalf as a monthly managed service, with optimisation, monitoring, and incident response built in.

### How it works

- **Audit** ($2,500–$7,500, 1–2 weeks) — paid diagnostic that maps your workflows, ranks AI opportunities, and produces a fixed-scope pilot proposal.
- **First Agent Pilot** ($10,000–$30,000, 2–4 weeks) — we build and run one workflow in shadow then approval mode, with measurable success criteria.
- **Managed Subscription** (from $5,000/month) — ongoing operation, optimisation, monthly reporting, and a roadmap for the next workflow.

### Where we work

`Trades` · `Mining services` · `Accounting` · `Legal admin` · `Property`

### What makes us different

- **The approval queue is the product.** Every customer-facing or system-changing action lands in front of a human before it ships.
- **Onshore by default.** Models run via Amazon Bedrock (Sydney) or Azure (Australia East). Your data stays in Australia and is documented per workflow.
- **One workflow at a time.** We refuse to sell five at once. The first one has to work before we propose the second.

### Trust

Every deployment ships with a human approval matrix, a model and provider register, structured audit logs (tool calls, approvals, rail trips, escalations), a documented risk tier, and a defined incident response process. Models run in Australian regions where supported. Customer data is not used to train provider models. The posture is plain Australian Privacy Principles compliance done well — not enterprise theatre.
