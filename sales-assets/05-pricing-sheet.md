## How we price

We price against the business value the workflow unlocks and the cost of the alternative — an additional admin hire, an operations manager, an AI consultant project, or the lost revenue and owner time the workflow recovers. We do not price by hour, by seat, or by API token. The pilot price is fixed before work starts; the subscription price is fixed before the month starts. Coastal-style audit examples and ROI estimates are on the website.

---

## Discounts and credits

- The audit fee is **credited against the pilot price** if the pilot is signed within 14 days of audit delivery.
- We do not discount the recurring subscription. Price is the price.
- We will reduce a first pilot price in exchange for a formal written case study and named testimonial — by negotiation, case-by-case.
